About Snap 'N' Stick

Founded by four students together during the year of 2023 to sell mainly stickers and posters which later on became to be known as Snap 'N' Stick as the demand for the products increased and increased. Taking the student community of yeshwanthpur campus by force, the team has even ventured to the central campus where the amount of sales has far exceeded the expectations of the team. Now a famous Snap 'N' Stick for its impressive quality at the cheapeast prices, the team is moving forward with the ambitious plan to broaden the scope of Snap 'N' Stick to make a name which stands for its quality, prices and modern designs.

Mission

To bring creativity to life by offering a diverse range of high-quality posters, stickers, and audio cards at affordable prices. We aim to inspire self-expression and create memorable moments through our unique and artistic products.

Vision

To become the go-to destination for individuals seeking exceptional and budget-friendly visual and auditory experiences. We envision a world where everyone can effortlessly enhance their surroundings with personalized, high-quality art that resonates with their individuality.
